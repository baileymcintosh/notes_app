Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

Voice Notes is designed to keep your data on your device. Here's exactly what happens:

  • Voice recordings are sent directly from your browser to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription. They are not stored on our servers.
  • Your OpenAI API key is stored only in your browser's local storage. It is never sent to our servers.
  • Note content is stored only in your browser's local storage on your device.
  • Google account access is obtained via OAuth 2.0. Your refresh token is stored in a secure HttpOnly cookie on our server (Netlify). We use it solely to create calendar events and tasks on your behalf. We do not read, store, or share your Google data.

Third-party services

  • OpenAI — transcription (Whisper) and classification (GPT-4o-mini). See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Google — Calendar and Tasks integration. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify — hosting and serverless functions. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.

Data retention

We do not retain any user data on our servers beyond the Google refresh token cookie, which is deleted when you disconnect Google or it expires (400 days).
